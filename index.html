    🐛 IPM Quick Reference

    🍂 Yellow/Brown Leaves

    Could be nutrient deficiency, fungal disease, or water stress. Check pH first.

    🪲 Stem Borer

    Look for dead hearts in paddy. Use pheromone traps + neem-based sprays early.

    🦟 Aphids / Whitefly

    Yellow sticky traps + neem oil 2ml/L spray. Avoid excess nitrogen fertiliser.

    🍄 Fungal Disease

    Improve airflow + reduce overhead irrigation. Apply copper-based fungicide early.

    📖 What is Crop Bot?

    Crop Bot is a free, AI-powered farming assistant designed to empower farmers across India and the world. It gives hyper-local, context-aware advice based on your crop, soil, location, weather, and even photos of your plants.

    ✨ Key Features

    • 🎤 Voice input & text-to-speech in 55+ languages
    • 📸 AI photo diagnosis for diseases, pests & deficiencies
    • 🌦️ Hyper-local weather alerts & irrigation timing
    • 💰 Mandi market price snapshot
    • 🧾 Government scheme guidance (PM-KISAN, PMFBY)
    • 🐛 IPM pest management quick reference
    • 👤 Personalised farm profile for tailored advice

    🏆 Built By a Student, For Farmers

    Crop Bot was designed and coded entirely by Ayushmaan Singh Pundir, a 12-year-old student from India, with a mission to bridge the technology gap in agriculture and make expert farming advice accessible to every farmer — in their own language, on any device, for free.
